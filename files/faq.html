Medications & safety

TideMedix currently presents GLP-1 weight care paths including Semaglutide and Tirzepatide options, with oral pill and injection-shot paths shown for education and assessment routing. Availability and appropriateness are determined through provider review.
TideMedix is built around online assessment, compliance-minded education, and licensed provider review before treatment decisions. Your complete health history matters, and not every person is a fit for GLP-1 medication.
Common side effects of GLP-1 medications may include nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, constipation, and stomach discomfort, especially when starting treatment. Your provider should discuss risks, contraindications, and monitoring with you if treatment is prescribed.
GLP-1 medications may not be appropriate for people who are pregnant or breastfeeding, have certain thyroid cancer risks, have Multiple Endocrine Neoplasia syndrome type 2, have a history of pancreatitis, have Type 1 diabetes, or have other health factors a provider determines are unsafe.
